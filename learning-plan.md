# Learning Plan

This plan is tailored for a learner with a background in AI, new to Web3, focused on Product Research, with 1.5 hours available daily.

## Phase 1: Web3 Fundamentals (Weeks 1-2)

- **Goal:** Build a solid understanding of core Web3 concepts.
- **Topics:**
    - [ ] **Cryptography:** Public/private keys, hashing.
    - [ ] **Wallets:** How they work, security best practices (e.g., MetaMask).
    - [ ] **Smart Contracts:** Basic principles, what they can do.
    - [ ] **Networks:** Mainnet vs. Testnet, Layers (L1/L2).
    - [ ] **DeFi:** Core concepts like DEX, lending, staking.
    - [ ] **Oracles:** The problem they solve.

## Phase 2: AI × Web3 Bridge (Weeks 3-4)

- **Goal:** Connect AI capabilities with Web3 infrastructure for product innovation.
- **Topics:**
    - [ ] **Chain-aware Context:** How can AI understand on-chain data?
    - [ ] **Web3 Tool Use:** How can Agents interact with dApps and wallets?
    - [ ] **Agent Workflows:** Designing flows for Web3 tasks.
    - [ ] **Agent Wallet & Machine Payments:** Exploring autonomous economic agents.
    - [ ] **AI Oracles:** Bringing AI-generated data on-chain.

## Phase 3: Product Research & Prototyping (Ongoing)

- **Goal:** Identify and analyze interesting use cases and product ideas.
- **Activities:**
    - [ ] Analyze 1-2 existing AI x Web3 projects per week.
    - [ ] Brainstorm and outline a product idea in `hackathon/`.
    - [ ] Follow developments in Agent Identity, Trust, and Security.
    - [ ] Document findings and ideas in `daily/` and `experiments/`.

---
*This is a living document and will be updated based on progress and interests.*
